Hi, I'm Nathan

I turn messages into results & visitors into customers.

Thank you for taking interest in me. Currently, I’m working with the fantastic people of Codelitt. I’ve worn many hats, but at the moment I’m responsible for all things marketing.

I’ve always been in customer-facing roles. I enjoy people and am passionate about what makes each one of us tick. Are you having a good day or a bad one? Why might that be? Can I make it better?

Empathy and curiosity are what I employ to get these answers. I've honed these two assets while traveling the world and through my diverse work experience. They make it possible for me to quickly distill complicated thoughts into simple ones.

At the end of the day, humans buy from humans. My job is to make sure that the humans that you want to impact have an effortless experience in understanding the concepts you want them to.
